Introduction
Building on your success with inbound marketing in Project 1, you'll now tackle the outbound marketing landscape. This project challenges you to develop a comprehensive paid media strategy, leveraging AI tools and automation to create and manage effective advertising campaigns across multiple platforms.

Project Goal
Design and implement a data-driven outbound marketing strategy for your product launch, incorporating paid search, social media advertising, and programmatic display. Your strategy should demonstrate mastery of paid channels while showcasing how AI and automation can enhance campaign performance and efficiency.
